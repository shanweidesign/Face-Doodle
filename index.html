Welcome to Project Face Doodle!

Your face is about to become the canvas for a digital arts, inspired by three unique artists. Leveraging real-time face detection technology, this interactive project invites you to see yourself reimagined through artistic filters.

Just a heads up, we will need to use your camera for this art show. Please allow your web browser permission to use your webcam. Ready to see your face turn into awesome digital art?
