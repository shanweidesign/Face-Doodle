Piet Mondrian
Composition with Yellow, Blue and Red. 1937-42

Piet Mondrian, a Dutch artist, revolutionized abstract art with his grid paintings. His work features bold geometric lines and primary colors, defining the De Stijl movement. Mondrian's grids convey harmony and the essential order of the universe, simplifying forms to their purest expressions.
